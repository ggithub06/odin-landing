Sons of Odin

Thor

a hammer-wielding god associated with lightning, thunder, storms, sacred groves and trees, strength, the protection of mankind, hallowing, and fertility.

Baldr

loved by all the gods, goddesses, and beings of a more physical nature. So handsome, gracious, and cheerful is he that he actually gives off light.

Víðarr

the silent, the possessor of the iron shoe, the enemy and slayer of Fenrir, the avenger, and he who inhabits the homestead of his father.

Váli

a personification of the lengthening days, he grew with such marvelous rapidity, that in the course of a single day he attained his full stature.
